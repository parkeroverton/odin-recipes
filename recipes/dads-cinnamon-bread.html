Dad's Cinnamon Bread

Aka "Amelia's Favorite"

Shown above is Amelia's favorite meal: The cinnamon bread that Dad makes her.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • A bit of butter
  • A dash of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Spread butter on the bread
  2. Sprinkle the 'dash' of cinnamon on the bread
  3. Cut into thin strips, and hand them to Amelia
